## Method notes

* Point estimates are the inverse-variance ensemble of a damped local linear trend (Holt's damped method, log space) and an AR(1) on log-differences.
* 90% CIs combine ACS sample standard error (MOE/1.645) and model forecast SE (Hyndman ETS(A,Ad,N) closed-form, with n/(n−2) small-sample correction and an empirical 1.30× calibration multiplier validated on walk-forward back-tests).
* Projections are capped at ±10%/yr compound growth — the annual analog of the ±0.0189/month CPI cap that governs the rest of this repo.
* Walk-forward back-tests on 2015-2022 anchors (96 folds, 4 counties × 4 indicators × 6 anchors, 2-year horizon) yielded median absolute percentage error 5.4% and 90%-CI coverage of 88.5% — see `backtests/results/backtest_*.md`.
