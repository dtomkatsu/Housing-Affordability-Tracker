## Reading the table
* **MAPE / medAPE**: lower is better. The ensemble should beat or match `carry_forward` and `linear_log` to justify its complexity.
* **Bias**: signed mean of pct error. >0 means the model systematically over-projects; <0 means under-projects.
* **CI90 cov**: fraction of folds whose actual fell inside the projected 90% interval. ~90% is well-calibrated; >>90% means intervals are wider than necessary; <<90% means intervals understate uncertainty.
